Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Avatar Studio

What is AI Avatar Studio?

+

AI Avatar Studio is a cutting-edge platform that creates realistic AI-powered avatar videos from text. Using advanced facial animation, lip-sync technology, and natural voice synthesis, it allows you to generate professional videos in minutes without cameras, actors, or video editing skills.

How does AI Avatar technology work?

+

Our platform combines three core technologies: (1) ElevenLabs AI voice synthesis to create natural-sounding speech from your text, (2) Wav2Lip technology for precise lip-sync animation, and (3) GPU-accelerated video rendering to composite your avatar with backgrounds and effects. The entire process happens automatically in minutes.

Who can benefit from using AI Avatar Studio?

+

Content creators, marketers, educators, business professionals, course creators, social media managers, and anyone who needs to create video content quickly. It's perfect for training videos, product demos, educational content, social media posts, presentations, and more.

Do I need any technical skills to use this?

+

No technical skills required! Our interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. Simply select an avatar, choose a voice, type or paste your script, and click generate. The platform handles all the complex processing automatically.

What are your pricing plans?

+

We offer flexible pricing tiers: Free (3 videos/month, 720p, watermark), Starter ($29/mo, 20 videos/month, 1080p, no watermark), Pro ($79/mo, 100 videos/month, 4K, priority processing, API access), and Enterprise (custom pricing, unlimited videos, dedicated support, white-label options).

Is there a free trial?

+

Yes! Our free plan includes 3 video generations per month at 720p resolution. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade anytime to access higher quality, more videos, and premium features.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with the service, contact us within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. No questions asked.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period, and no further charges will be made.

What are the technical requirements?

+

AI Avatar Studio is web-based and works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Recommended: stable internet connection (5+ Mbps), and a device with at least 4GB RAM for smooth playback of generated videos.

What video quality options are available?

+

We support three quality tiers: 720p HD (fast processing, smaller file size), 1080p Full HD (recommended, best balance), and 4K Ultra HD (maximum quality, longer processing). Choose based on your needs and plan.

How long does video generation take?

+

Processing time depends on video length and quality: 720p (~1-2 minutes for a 30-second video), 1080p (~2-4 minutes), 4K (~5-10 minutes). Pro and Enterprise plans get priority processing for faster results.

What are the file size limits?

+

Script length: up to 10,000 characters per video. Avatar uploads: max 10MB, JPEG/PNG format. Background uploads: max 20MB. Generated video duration: up to 10 minutes per video on Pro/Enterprise plans, 2 minutes on Free/Starter.

What export formats do you support?

+

All videos are exported as MP4 (H.264 codec) for maximum compatibility. Pro users also get access to WebM, MOV formats, and can extract audio as MP3/WAV. Videos are optimized for social media platforms.

Can I customize the avatars?

+

Yes! Upload your own photo to create a custom avatar, or choose from our library of 50+ pre-made avatars. Customize backgrounds, adjust speaking speed, select from 30+ voice options, and fine-tune facial expressions and gestures.

Does it support voice cloning?

+

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include voice cloning. Upload 1-2 minutes of clear audio of your voice, and our system will create a custom voice model that sounds like you. Perfect for personal branding and consistency.

What languages are supported?

+

We support 30+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, and more. Each language has multiple voice options with native accents.

Can I use custom backgrounds?

+

Absolutely! Upload your own background images or videos, choose from our library of professional backgrounds (office, studio, outdoor scenes), or use a solid color/gradient. Supports transparent backgrounds for green screen effects.

Is there an API for developers?

+

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include full REST API access. Integrate video generation into your applications, automate workflows, and build custom solutions. Comprehensive documentation, SDKs for Python/Node.js/PHP, and webhook support included.

Can I generate multiple videos at once?

+

Yes! Batch processing is available on all plans. Queue up multiple videos with different scripts and settings, and they'll process automatically. Pro/Enterprise users can process up to 10 videos simultaneously.

Do you offer subtitles/captions?

+

Yes! Automatic subtitle generation is available in all supported languages. Choose from multiple caption styles, customize colors and fonts, and download SRT files separately. Accessibility-compliant and perfect for social media.

+

Yes! All paid plans include full commercial usage rights. You own the videos you create and can use them for marketing, advertising, sales, courses, social media, YouTube, and any other commercial applications without additional fees.

Who owns the generated content?

How do you protect my data and privacy?

+

We take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. Videos are stored securely and only accessible to you. We never share your content with third parties. GDPR and CCPA compliant. You can delete your data anytime.

What happens if video generation fails?

+

If generation fails, the credit is automatically returned to your account. Common issues include invalid scripts or file format problems. Our system provides detailed error messages, and support is available to help troubleshoot any issues.

How can I get support if I need help?

+

Multiple support channels available: Email support (24-48 hour response), Live chat for Pro/Enterprise users (business hours), comprehensive documentation and video tutorials, community forum, and the floating help button on this page for quick assistance.

Can I edit videos after generation?

+

While you can't edit the generated video directly in our platform, you can download and edit in any video editor. However, you can regenerate with different settings, scripts, or avatars anytime. Pro users get unlimited regenerations.

+

Can I download videos in bulk?

+

Yes! Pro and Enterprise users can select multiple videos from their library and download as a ZIP file. API users can also programmatically download videos via our RESTful endpoints.
